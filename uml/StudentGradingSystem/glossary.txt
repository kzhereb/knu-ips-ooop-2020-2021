Key entities for Student Grading System project

Teacher - responsible for checking open questions
Supervisor - present in classroom where the test takes place, checks for plagiarism/cheating/...,
Multiple choice questions - test questions with a list of answers, students have to choose correct answers from this list
Open questions - test questions without predefined answers, students have to write their own answer with explanations 
Test - a full set of questions, including multiple choice and open questions
Subject - e.g. mathematics, history, ...
Chosen subject - subject chosen by a student, also includes difficulty grade
Difficulty grade - whether chosen subject is main (more complex tasks) or additional (less complex tasks)
Test location - a place where testing event takes place (usually some university building)
